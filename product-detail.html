Product Preview

Sample Task 2 Template

Here's a preview of one of the Task 2 templates included in the course:

Agree/Disagree Essay Template

This template is designed for questions that ask whether you agree or disagree with a statement.

Introduction:

In recent years, [topic] has become a subject of intense debate. While some people argue that [opposing view], I firmly believe that [your view]. This essay will discuss the reasons for my position.

Body Paragraph 1:

First and foremost, [first reason]. [Explanation with example].

Body Paragraph 2:

Secondly, [second reason]. [Explanation with example].

Body Paragraph 3:

Finally, [third reason]. [Explanation with example].

Conclusion:

In conclusion, [restate your position]. [Brief summary of reasons].
