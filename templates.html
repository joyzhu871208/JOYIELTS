How do I access my purchased course or template?

After purchase, you'll receive an email with download instructions. For courses, you'll get access to our learning platform. For templates, you'll receive a Notion template link.

No. We do not offer refunds. However, you can contact us to discuss any issues or concerns.

Are the templates compatible with my Notion account?

Yes, all our templates are compatible with any Notion account. You'll receive a link to duplicate the template to your own workspace.
